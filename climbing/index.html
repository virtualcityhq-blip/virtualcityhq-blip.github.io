FAQ

What are Sendlines climbing prints?
Steel-blue engraving prints of real Colorado climbing routes — every line traced from published climbing documentation. Available in two sizes ($45 and $65) on Etsy. Climbing areas covered: Clear Creek Canyon, Boulder Canyon, and Staunton State Park.
What makes Sendlines prints different?
REAL ROUTE LINE. REAL PLACE. Every route line is based on real climbing documentation and community beta. Nothing invented, nothing approximated. The engraving style is AI-assisted but every route line is human-sourced.
Is AI used in making the prints?
The engraving style is AI-assisted: a human researcher documented each route from published sources; AI rendered the fine-line etching from that description. Every route line is traced from real documentation, not generated. Disclosed on every product page.
Where do the prints ship from?
Made to order in the US. Free US shipping. See individual Etsy listings for full details.
Which climbing areas are covered?
Three Colorado climbing areas: Clear Creek Canyon (6 routes, gneiss), Boulder Canyon (8 routes, granite), and Staunton State Park (7 routes, granite domes). 21 routes total.
