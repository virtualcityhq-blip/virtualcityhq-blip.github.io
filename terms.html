These terms govern Sendlines' internal social-publishing integrations. They do not govern purchases made through Etsy.

An operator may authorize only an account or channel that the operator has permission to control. Authorization and revocation.

Google OAuth access is voluntary and may be revoked at any time through Google Security Settings. Sendlines may suspend the integration immediately if authorization, account identity, permissions, compliance status, or publication verification is uncertain.

Privacy and security.

Data handling is described in the Sendlines privacy policy. Credentials must remain in approved secure storage and may not be placed in public source code, chat, screenshots, or audit submissions.

Availability.

The integrations are provided for internal operational use without a promise of uninterrupted availability. Publishing stops when a provider, authorization, policy, verification, safety, or compliance gate fails.

Changes and termination.

Sendlines may revise or discontinue an integration to remain compliant with provider requirements or business needs. Continued authorization after a posted revision constitutes acceptance by the authorized internal operator. Revocation or discontinuation ends future API access.
