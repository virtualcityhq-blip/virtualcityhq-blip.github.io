CUSTOMER CARE

Returns and exchanges.

Each Sendlines print is produced to order by our U.S. production partner. We accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of delivery, consistent with the policy shown at checkout in our Etsy shop.

Start a return or exchange.

Contact Sendlines through your Etsy order within 30 days of delivery before sending anything back. Items should be returned in their original condition. For non-defective returns or exchanges, the buyer is responsible for return shipping.

Damaged, defective, or incorrect items.

If an order arrives damaged, defective, or incorrect, contact us through Etsy with the order number and clear photos of the item and packaging. We will arrange a replacement or refund at no additional cost.

No surprise fees for fulfillment problems. Sendlines covers the resolution when the item is damaged, defective, or not what was ordered.

Cancellations.

Request cancellation through Etsy within 12 hours of ordering. Because items enter production quickly, cancellation may not be possible after production begins.

Refunds and contact.

Approved refunds are processed through the original Etsy order and payment method. Use the message or help-request controls on your Etsy order so the order details stay attached to the request.
